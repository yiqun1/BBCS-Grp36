Spread joy this festive season.

GenerosiTree is a community project that allows anyone to make and fulfill holiday wish requests.

This Christmas, let’s come together to spread Generosity, build Community, and embrace Festivity.

Be someone’s Santa by fulfilling heartfelt requests, taking on fun challenges, or making a small donation for a good cause.

Every act of kindness, big or small, brings us one step closer to lighting up Singapore—one smile, one gift, and one connection at a time.

Join us in creating a brighter, warmer holiday season for everyone. 🌟
